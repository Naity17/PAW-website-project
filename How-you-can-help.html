Sponsor or foster an animal

Even though we are trying our best to provide our strays with a safe and spacious environment in The Farm, there is not always enough space for all animals in need. Some of them require special care or just more calm environment to recover. Therefore, we are eagerly accepting volunteers willing to foster temporarily some of our animals until we could find forever homes for them.

If this is not an option for you but you are still willing to help a particular stray, you can become a virtual sponsor. This would mean that you would be taking care of the bills for food, medical supplies and whatever else you'd like to spoil with your chosen friend.

Have a look at our animals and let us know if one of them managed to melt your heart and you'd be interested to foster or sponsor it.
